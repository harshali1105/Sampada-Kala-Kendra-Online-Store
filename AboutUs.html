About US

Namaste Everyone!! I am Sanjay Dabholkar, the founder and Art Director of Sampada Kala Kendra. Since childhood, I have been deeply connected to the art and spirituality that surround Lord Ganesha. I started this journey with a vision to keep our traditions alive while protecting the environment.

At Sampada Kala Kendra, every Ganesha idol is lovingly handcrafted using red soil or clay (shadu). This decision wasn’t just about tradition—it’s my way of giving back to nature. I’ve seen how plaster-of-Paris idols harm our water bodies, and I felt a strong need to change that. By using eco-friendly materials, we ensure that the idols dissolve easily in water without causing pollution.

Every idol we create tells a story of devotion, creativity, and sustainability. Our team of talented artisans works tirelessly to bring unique designs to life, each one reflecting the beauty of our cultural heritage. Whether it’s for Ganesh Chaturthi or a special occasion, I want every home to feel the divine presence of Lord Ganesha in a way that respects both tradition and our planet.

When you choose Sampada Kala Kendra, you’re not just bringing home a Ganesha idol—you’re joining me in a movement towards eco-friendly celebrations.
